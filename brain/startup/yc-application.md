## YC Interview Prep (If Selected)

### Key Questions to Prepare
- What's the #1 thing that makes this win?
- Who are your first 10 customers and how will you get them?
- What's the growth plan after YC?
- How do you handle the cold-start problem (no tenants = no proof)?
- What's the moat?
- Why can't Wix/Squarespace just add real-estate features?
- What are the unit economics?
- How big can this get?

### Strong Answers to Develop
- **Moat**: Deep real-estate domain integration that generic builders can't replicate. Network effects as templates, AI models, and section library improve with usage.
- **Growth**: Direct sales to agencies in Lagos, partnerships with real-estate associations, word-of-mouth from published tenant sites acting as marketing.
- **Why now**: African real estate is formalizing, companies are moving online, AI makes content generation viable for non-technical users.
