## One-Liner
PlotKeys is the operating system for real-estate companies in Africa — one platform to manage operations, generate professional websites, and serve customers with AI-powered workflows.

## Problem
Real-estate companies in Africa operate with fragmented tools: separate systems for listings, websites, CRM, payments, and customer management. Most rely on social media and WhatsApp with no professional web presence. Companies that do have websites use generic builders that don't understand real estate, leading to poor lead conversion and manual operations.

## Solution
A vertical SaaS platform purpose-built for real-estate companies that combines:
- AI-powered website generation with real-estate-specific templates
- Property listing and agent management
- Lead capture and CRM
- Customer portal with payments and estate management
- All in a multi-tenant architecture where each company gets their own branded experience

## Market
- **TAM**: African real estate market (~$1.4T asset value, growing)
- **SAM**: Nigerian real-estate companies, developers, and agents (~50,000+ registered firms)
- **SOM**: Initial target — mid-market agencies and developers in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt (~5,000 companies)
- NOTE: These figures need validation with primary research

## Business Model
- Monthly SaaS subscriptions (Starter NGN 24,000/mo, Plus, Pro)
- AI credit packs (content generation, property descriptions, SEO)
- Paid template purchases and upgrades
- Custom domain provisioning fees
- Future: transaction fees on customer payments processed through the platform

## Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | What They Do | PlotKeys Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| Generic builders (Wix, Squarespace) | General-purpose websites | Not real-estate-specific, no CRM/listings integration |
| PropTech platforms (global) | Property portals (Zillow, Rightmove) | Consumer-facing marketplaces, not B2B operating system |
| Local listing sites (Nigeria Property Centre, etc.) | Listing aggregators | No company ops, no branded websites, no customer management |
| Custom development | Bespoke solutions | Expensive, slow, no shared platform benefits |

## Key Risks Investors Will Ask About
- **Market risk**: Will real-estate companies in Nigeria pay for SaaS tools?
- **Execution risk**: Solo founder / small team building a complex platform
- **Competition risk**: What stops a well-funded competitor from copying this?
- **Regulatory risk**: Payment processing, data protection in Nigeria
- **Unit economics**: Can customer acquisition cost be recovered within reasonable payback period?
